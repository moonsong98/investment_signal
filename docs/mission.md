# Mission

Build a personal investment research and alert agent that helps a human investor
study markets, monitor important signals, log events, and draft research notes.

The system must protect core capital. It should help with awareness and research,
not outsource investment judgment.

## Operating Principles

- 90% of capital is treated as protected core capital for cash or long-term investing.
- Up to 10% may later be used for paper trading and small experimental automation.
- v0 through v3 must not include live trading or broker API order placement.
- LLMs may summarize, classify, explain risks, and draft notes.
- LLMs must not make final buy or sell decisions.
- All final investment decisions are made by a human.
