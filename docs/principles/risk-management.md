# Risk Management

- 90% of capital is protected core capital.
- Up to 10% may later be used for paper trading and small automation experiments.
- v0 through v3 contain no live trading.
- v4 contains paper trading only.
- v5 requires a separate plan and explicit approval.

## Future Automation Guardrails

- Start with paper trading.
- Require maximum position size and maximum daily loss.
- Require duplicate-order protection.
- Require an emergency stop.
- Require audit logs for every generated action.
