# Agent Roles

## Allowed

- Monitor watchlists and major market indicators.
- Normalize and classify alert events.
- Send Telegram notifications based on severity.
- Log structured events.
- Summarize important events.
- Draft research notes and journal entries.

## Not Allowed

- Make final investment decisions.
- Place live orders.
- Connect broker APIs in v0 through v3.
- Publish secrets or personal account details.
- Treat LLM output as financial advice.
