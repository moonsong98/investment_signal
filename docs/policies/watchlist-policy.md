# Watchlist Policy

Each watchlist item should state why it is monitored and what would invalidate
or strengthen the thesis.

Recommended fields:
- `symbol`
- `name`
- `asset_type`
- `market`
- `currency`
- `thesis`
- `risk_notes`
- `tags`
- `priority`
- `active`

The watchlist should include companies, ETFs, indices, FX pairs, commodities,
and macro indicators only when they serve a defined research purpose.
